Nathan Jacobs

I am an Associate Professor of Computer Science at the University of Kentucky, Director of the Multimodal Vision Research Laboratory at the University of Kentucky, and the owner of Multidomain Vision Research.

I earned a Ph.D. in Computer Science @ Washington University in St. Louis, under the direction of Robert Pless.

Research

I am excited about using computer vision and machine learning techniques to understand the world.
